SoulCraft

July 2014 - September 2014

SoulCraft is a video game modification I created for the popular game minecraft. Programmed from scratch, re-using and adding to stock minecraft code, I created soulcraft to further my understanding of object oriented programming and learn java.

Soulcraft gained the attention of many players in the Minecraft community and has very positive reviews. It has been downladed more than 40,000 times and has been played 1.8 million times to date.

Youtube Reviews

Soulcraft has been reviewed by many minecraft players on youtube. The most popular videos however are by YouTubers Sundee and PopularMMO's with both videos having more than 1 million views.
